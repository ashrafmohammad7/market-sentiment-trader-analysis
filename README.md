# AI Internship Task

## Objective

Analyze trader behavior using Bitcoin Fear & Greed market sentiment data.

https://drive.google.com/file/d/1IAfLZwu6rJzyWKgBToqwSmmVYU6VbjVs/view?usp=sharing

### Fear & Greed Index Data

## Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Analysis Performed

1. Data loading
2. Data cleaning
3. Dataset merging
4. Profitability analysis
5. Trading activity analysis
6. Volume analysis
7. Visualization

## Key Insights

- Market sentiment affects trading activity.
- Greed periods show higher trading activity.
- Fear sentiment results in cautious trading behavior.
- Trading volume varies according to sentiment.

## Hidden Patterns Observed

- Traders were more active during Greed sentiment periods.
- Fear sentiment showed reduced trading activity and lower risk appetite.
- Trading profitability fluctuated depending on market sentiment.
- Market sentiment appears to influence trader decision-making behavior.

## Strategy Insights

- Traders may reduce leverage during Fear markets to minimize risk.
- Greed phases may provide higher trading opportunities but also higher volatility.
- Sentiment indicators can be combined with trading signals for better risk management.
